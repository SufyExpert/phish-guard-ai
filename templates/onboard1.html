PhishGuard AI

How Our AI Works

A three-stage machine learning pipeline that analyses every email for phishing threats with precision.

01
Email Text Input
Raw email text is ingested and cleaned — URLs, HTML tags, and noise are stripped to preserve meaningful content.
02
TF-IDF Vectorization
Text is transformed into a 40,000-feature numerical matrix using TF-IDF with unigrams and bigrams to capture language patterns.
03
Logistic Regression
A trained classifier predicts phishing probability with 98.26% accuracy and generates a confidence score.
